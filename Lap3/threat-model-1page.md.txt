PHAN TICH MOI DE DOA (THREAT MODEL) - LAB 3

1. Tai san can bao ve (Assets):
- Noi dung tin nhan goc (Plaintext).
- Khoa giai ma DES (Key) va Vector khoi tao (IV).

2. Cac moi de doa (Threats):
- Nghe len (Sniffing): Ke tan cong su dung cong cu nhu Wireshark trong cung mang LAN de bat goi tin. Vi Key va IV duoc gui cong khai o dau goi tin, ke tan cong co the lay duoc chung de giai ma tin nhan.
- Tan cong xen giua (Man-in-the-Middle): Ke tan cong thay doi noi dung ban ma hoac gia mao Header khien Receiver nhan sai du lieu hoac bi treo.

3. Bien phap giam thieu (Mitigation):
- Su dung ma hoa bat doi xung (RSA) de trao doi khoa.
- Trien khai giao thuc bao mat SSL/TLS cho Socket.
